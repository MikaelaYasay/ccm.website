Picket Fencing

About Picket Fencing

Picket fencing is a great way to keep pets as well as children in the yard and keeps them out of harm’s way. It adds warmth to a landscape and doesn’t come off as too aggressive. This type of fencing is also a great match if you live in an area with heavy winds due to the spacing between the boards.

Picket Fencing sizes

  • 6’ x 2.5” (#1)
  • 6’ x 3” (#1)
  • 6’ x 2” (mill grade)
  • 6’ x 2.5” (#2)
  • 6’ x 3” (#2)

About Post & Rails

Hand-split post and rails are an ideal choice if you are going for an informal country look. Our post and rail fences have two or three rails that slide right into a post, making it easy for installation.
