Cedar Creek Manufacturing

As a small, local, and very competitive fencing manufacturer in Weippe, Idaho, we set out to make high-quality, hand-split cedar post and rail fences, rough “rustic” pickets, and custom-cut cedar fence boards products at affordable prices. Lately, we have discovered this market to be overly priced. So we have started an advertising campaign to let the world know that Cedar Creek Manufacturing has those fence boards that you’ve been dreaming of at a price any working man or woman feels is fair to pay.
